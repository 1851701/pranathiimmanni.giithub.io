1.2.5 Project

This was my groups porject, the idea was to create a game where each person has to go across five lanes, without getting hit and going by any speed that you prefer. If you reach the big box at the top you get a point, but if you get hit by a car then you don't get any points and get back to the bottom to restart because you lost.
